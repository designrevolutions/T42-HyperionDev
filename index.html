The Nitty Gritty Details

We get 100 people who want to make a startup - to sell online ‘something’ - can be absolutely anything.

The people can be existing businesses. They can even have their own websites - but they want to move to the next level. They could be people who’ve had businesses in the past and have an immense amount of business experience.

We then use online bootcamps to develop the ecommerce websites. Every session, we make an advancement and move onto the next level of development. If someone has trouble implementing, they can get help from the group. If they can’t accomplish a task, then they can pay to have that part complete.

Oath of Allegiance

Each member takes an oath of allegiance to help promote and make active contributions to help promote the 100 businesses.

In life, if you roll dice, you have 1 in 6 chances of winning. Our mission is to cheat this system and roll 100 dice. For those where we don’t get a 6, we roll again and again and again until we ‘get lucky’.

Regular Meetings

We’ll have regular meetings. Each person is expected to attend. Failure to attend 3 times in a row means they lose their place. They might have genuine excuses, but 3 times in a row and they’re out. This applies to experts as well.

No Secrets

The experts need to give their exact formulas used and explain why they have succeeded in the past.

100 Voters for Experts

The experts have to pitch themselves. The ‘100 bootcampers’ need to vote for experts before they are accepted into the business.

Delay and You're Out

Each of the 100 startups need to keep up progress in developing their websites. More than 3 delays and they're out - no refunds.
